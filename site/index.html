From trace to evidence

01

Diagnose

Detect resource mismatch, noisy results, evaluator drift, confounded patches, and unsupported claims.

02

Probe

Choose the cheapest next check: seed-group comparison, frozen evaluator, ablation, or claim reconstruction.

03

Gate

Allow robust claims, rewrite inconclusive claims, and avoid follow-up probes whose expected value is low.
